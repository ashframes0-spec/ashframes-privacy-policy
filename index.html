Privacy Policy

Welcome to AshFrames ("the App"). Your privacy and trust mean everything to us. We want you to feel completely safe and secure while using our app to bring your animations to life. This policy explains how we respect and protect your privacy.

100% Offline & Private

AshFrames is a fully offline application. This means all your animation creation, project rendering, and file processing happen strictly on your local device. The App does not require or use an internet connection to function, ensuring your creative workspace is entirely private.

Information We Do Not Collect

Because the App operates completely offline, we do not collect, store, or track any of your personal information, account details, or usage data. Everything you create stays safely with you.

Permissions We Request

To give you the best animation experience, we only request the following essential permissions:

  • Camera – This is used only when you choose to use our built-in camera color picker tool. Please rest assured that no images or videos are ever captured, stored, or shared.
  • Storage / Media Access – This is used only to let you freely import your own images, audio tracks, or 3D models from your device into your animation projects. All your creative files remain strictly on your own device.

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any of your data and the App runs offline, we absolutely do not share any information with third parties or advertisers. Your work is entirely your own.

We Are Always Here For You

If you have any friendly questions, suggestions, or just want to reach out, please feel free to drop us an email at: ashframes0@gmail.com
